## 5. Logo Usage Guidelines

The logo represents the letter **"M"** formed by a **circuit board** merging with a **human brain**.

-   **Do**: Use the logo on dark backgrounds.
-   **Do**: Allow the logo to glow (CSS `box-shadow`).
-   **Don't**: Invert the colors (it loses the neon effect).
-   **Don't**: Place on busy, light backgrounds without a backing plate.
